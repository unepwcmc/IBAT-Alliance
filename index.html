The Integrated Biodiversity Assessment Tool (IBAT) is a unique portal through which trusted, global information on biodiversity can be accessed to support decision-makers in the public and private sectors

The IBAT Alliance was formed by four leading global conservation organisations: BirdLife International, Conservation International, IUCN and UN Environment's World Conservation Monitoring Centre. The Alliance was formed to enable businesses, governments and researchers to better understand their relationships with people and nature.

For business users

Better insight into risks and opportunities through rapid screening of critical biodiversity features by site using high-quality data, direct from the authority source.

Explore IBAT for Business

For financial safeguard compliance

Easily gather the data and information required for World Bank Group, Equator Principle Financial Institutions and Export Credit Agencies to manage compliance with IFC Performance Standard 6.

Visit IBAT for Finance

For research and conservation planning

Access country data summaries which support National Biodiversity Strategies and Action Plans and explore integrated global biodiversity data for research. Available for non-commercial use only.

View IBAT for Research and Conservation Planning

IBAT is the only online tool which delivers global biodiversity data layers direct from the authority sources:

  • IUCN Red List of Threatened Species
  • Key Biodiversity Areas managed by BirdLife International
  • World Database on Protected Areas maintained by UNEP-WCMC
