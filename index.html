IBAT provides decision-makers across the private and not-for-profit sectors with access to critical information on biodiversity priority sites enabling users to assess biodiversity impacts and inform decision-making processes

Using key conservation datasets provided by some of the world’s leading global biodiversity organisations, the Integrated Biodiversity Assessment Tool (IBAT) enables businesses, governments and researchers to access a range of global data layers critical to assessing risk and informing land use planning decisions.

  • IUCN Red List of Threatened Species
  • Key Biodiversity Areas
  • World Database on Protected Areas
